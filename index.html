Solid Planet Structure Modeling

A project that models the structure of solid planets. A 4th order Runge Kutta method is used to solve differential equations that describe the relationships between pressure, density, and mass in solid planets. A planet made of iron is used as an example in this project. By solving the equations, graphs of radius vs mass, density, and pressure are produced to visualize planet structure.
